Frequently asked questions.

How can my organization benefit from an AI automation platform?

An AI automation platform can transform the way your organization operates by enabling faster workflows, reducing manual effort, and significantly increasing productivity.
By automating repetitive tasks, streamlining complex processes, and integrating seamlessly with your existing systems, it allows your teams to focus on strategic initiatives.
Whether it’s optimizing document processing, improving decision-making, or scaling operations without adding headcount, intelligent automation delivers fast ROI and positions your organization for growth in a competitive landscape.

What makes Altilia different from API-based AI tools or copilots?

Altilia goes beyond personal productivity tools by delivering enterprise-scale automation. Unlike API-based solutions or copilots, our platform creates custom AI agents that deeply understand your business processes.
These agents integrate seamlessly with your existing systems, operate autonomously at scale, and ensure compliance with industry standards.
With Altilia, you can automate entire workflows across departments, not just isolated tasks—unlocking greater efficiency, consistency, and ROI for your organization.

What is Agentic AI, and why does Altilia use it?

Agentic AI refers to the ability to orchestrate complex workflows that solve multi-step problems by combining data classification, extraction, manipulation, inference, and actions.
Altilia uses Agentic AI to go beyond simple task automation. These agents deeply understand business processes, automate sophisticated workflows, and deliver accurate, context-aware results.
By leveraging Agentic AI, Altilia ensures enterprises can achieve greater efficiency, scalability, and intelligent decision-making across their operations.

How customized are Altilia's AI agents to my business?

Each AI agent is tailored specifically for your organization, trained on your actual documents and data. They learn your unique processes and terminology, becoming increasingly accurate through continuous feedback.
It's like having a digital workforce who has been trained extensively with your company's knowledge and practices.

What types of processes can Altilia automate?

Altilia can automate a vast range of business processes across industries, from simple document handling to complex decision-making workflows. The platform is designed to identify patterns in data and processes, making it ideal for:

Document Processing: Extracting, classifying, and structuring data from contracts, financial reports, regulatory documents, and more.
Workflow Automation: Streamlining end-to-end processes like lending, underwriting, and procurement.
Regulatory Compliance: Tracking and analysing regulations to ensure adherence and automate reporting.
Customer Service: Managing high volumes of inquiries, automating responses, and routing issues efficiently.
Healthcare and Legal Applications: Transforming clinical records, automating billing and coding, and simplifying legal document review.

Whether it's financial analysis, supply chain optimization, or public administration workflows, Altilia adapts to your specific needs, delivering intelligent automation at scale.

How does Altilia integrate with our existing systems?

Altilia integrates seamlessly with your existing systems, thanks to pre-built connectors for common business applications such as CRM, ERP, and ECM platforms. For custom needs, robust APIs ensure smooth integration with other tools and workflows.
The platform offers flexible deployment options, including SaaS, private clouds, or on-premises, making it adaptable to your infrastructure requirements and ensuring a frictionless implementation process.

How does Altilia ensure the security of data?

Altilia ensures robust data security through its Integrated Management System (SGI), aligned with ISO/IEC 27001, ISO/IEC 27017, and ISO/IEC 27018 standards. These certifications validate that Altilia adheres to best practices for information security and cloud-based data protection.

Key measures include:

  • Risk Management: Regular assessment and mitigation of security risks.
  • Access Controls: Role-based permissions to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Incident Response: Procedures to identify, address, and contain security incidents.

With continuous monitoring, audits, and updates, Altilia ensures the confidentiality, integrity, and availability of your data, delivering enterprise-grade security and compliance.
